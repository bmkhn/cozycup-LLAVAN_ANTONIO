ABOUT US

Our Story

What started as a shared love for coffee and cooking turned into this cozy cafe. We’re here to share our passion for great food, great drinks, and great company with you.

Julius Vincent D. Llavan

Role: Coffee Guru

Passionate about crafting the perfect espresso shot. When not at the cafe, you'll find them reading about coffee science or hunting for new blends.

Diane Nicole R. Antonio

Role: Culinary Magician

A self-taught chef who dreams up all the delicious pastries and dishes. They believe every bite should feel like a warm hug.

Our Mission

At CozyCup, our mission is to create a welcoming space where everyone feels at home. Whether it’s through the perfect cup of coffee, a homemade treat, or a friendly chat, we’re dedicated to making your day a little brighter, one visit at a time.

Fun Fact About Us

We’re best known for debating whether coffee or pastries are the heart of the cafe (we know the answer is both).
